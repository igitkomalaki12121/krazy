INITAO COLLEGE

BEED - Bachelor of Elementary Education

The BEED program prepares future educators with the skills and knowledge necessary to teach in elementary schools, emphasizing inclusive education and foundational learning.

BSCRIM - Bachelor of Science in Criminology

The BSCRIM program equips students with criminological expertise, law enforcement training, and critical thinking for careers in public safety, corrections, and criminal investigation.

BSHM - Bachelor of Science in Hospitality Management

The BSHM program nurtures students' abilities in hospitality operations, customer service, and tourism industry trends, fostering future professionals in hotels and event management.

BSBA - Bachelor of Science in Business Administration

The BSBA program empowers students with essential business, management, and entrepreneurial skills to succeed in both corporate and startup environments.
